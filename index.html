Water Damage

Have you ever dropped a device in water? A common misconception is that you should leave it out to dry overnight, or you might even plug it in right away to test the charger. The truth is that both of these methods are not only ineffective, but actually dangerous for your device. Water is a conductor and electronic components don't like that. Leaving your device to sit for several hours just gives the motherboard time to soak. And plugging a charger in while the port is wet could short several components, essentially frying your device! Here at iRepair, we employ industry-standard methods for water damage and use state-of-the-art BAKU Ultrasonic Cleaners in our treatment process.
